Find us on the map

SnoBum currently has only two storefronts in the Tahoe area. Our Tahoe City location delivers to the North Shore Lake Tahoe Area, including Incline Village, Olympic Valley, and Tahoma, while our Stateline location delivers along the South Shore, including Meeks Bay, Glenbrook, and Heavenly.

SnoBum is preparing to expand into Colorado. We are looking into storefront locations in Aspen and Breckenridge, with plans to open our doors for the 2022-2023 Snow Season.
